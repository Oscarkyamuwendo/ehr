EHR Information

The Electronic Health Record (EHR) system allows doctors to manage patient data such as:

  • Demographics
  • Medical history
  • Medication and allergies
  • Immunization status
  • Laboratory test results
  • Radiology images
  • Vital signs
  • Personal statistics like age and weight
  • Billing information
